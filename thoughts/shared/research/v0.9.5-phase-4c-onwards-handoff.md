1. **`STATIC_DIR` clearing at import** (`backend/open_webui/config.py:927-936`) — importing `open_webui.main` deletes everything in `backend/open_webui/static/` and re-copies from `build/`. When there's no frontend build the tracked files just disappear from the worktree. Not a bug we introduced (upstream behavior), but easy to surprise the next person. Long-term you might want a `if FRONTEND_BUILD_DIR.exists():` guard.
