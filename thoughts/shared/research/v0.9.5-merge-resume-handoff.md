## Top-of-funnel critical finding (BLOCKER for ship)

The v0.9.0 async refactor turned many model methods async (`Chats.delete_chat_by_id`, `Feedbacks.delete_feedbacks_by_user_id`, `Notes.delete_notes_by_user_id`, `OAuthSessions.delete_sessions_by_user_id`, dozens of `get_*`/`update_*`/`delete_*` methods across models).

Our **`services/{deletion,retention,archival,export}/service.py`** are sync but call these without `await` — post-merge they will silently produce un-awaited coroutines that do nothing. This breaks **GDPR-critical features**: user deletion, data retention TTL cleanup, archival.

**Decision (Lex 2026-05-25):** add a Phase 1.5 between Phase 1 conflict resolution and the merge commit. Convert these services to async, update their callers (schedulers + admin routes). Substantial work — own plan.
